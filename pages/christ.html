Christ the Redeemer

Overview Christ the Redeemer is an iconic statue of Jesus Christ that overlooks Rio de Janeiro. It symbolizes faith, peace, and unity, standing tall with open arms.
History Designed by engineer Heitor da Silva Costa and completed in 1931, the 30-meter-tall monument was built using reinforced concrete and soapstone. It was declared one of the New Seven Wonders of the World in 2007.
Location Rio de Janeiro, Brazil (Mount Corcovado)
